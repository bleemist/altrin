The ALTRIN Story

ALTRIN was born from a simple yet powerful belief: that every child, regardless of their background, deserves a clear path from primary school to university graduation — supported by engaged parents, dedicated teachers, and a thriving community.

What started as a conversation among passionate educators and community leaders in Buliisa District has grown into a movement that has impacted over 500 learners across 10+ schools. Our approach is holistic — addressing not just academic needs, but also the economic and environmental factors that shape a child's future.

The name ALTRIN — short for Albertine Transformation Initiative — reflects our commitment to the Albertine region of Uganda, where we are working to unlock the full potential of our communities through education, empowerment, and environmental stewardship.

Our journey

Milestones

A timeline of ALTRIN's growth and impact since our founding in 2021.

2021

Foundation

ALTRIN is founded in Buliisa District with a vision to transform lives through education and community engagement. The first radio talk show on Biiso FM 98.7 creates public awareness.

2022

First Programs

Launch of Learner Tracking Systems and Career Guidance programs in partnership with local secondary schools. Initial outreach to 5 schools in Buliisa District.

2023

Expansion

TaRL and Mentorship programs introduced. First Inter-Secondary Schools Debate & Poem Launch held. Partnerships grow to 10+ schools.

2024-2025

Growth & Impact

Parent Economic Empowerment and Environmental Stewardship programs added. Reached 500+ learners across 10+ schools. 20+ teachers trained in TaRL methodology.

What guides us

Our Core Values

Six principles that drive everything we do at ALTRIN.

Community First

We believe in the power of collective action. Our work is driven by and for the communities we serve. Every decision we make prioritizes the well-being and empowerment of the people we work with.

Excellence

We strive for academic and professional excellence in everything we do — from our programs to our partnerships. We believe that quality education is the foundation of sustainable development.

Partnership

We build lasting alliances with schools, local governments, and organizations to amplify our impact. We believe that collaboration is the key to creating sustainable change.

Sustainability

We are committed to long-term impact through economic empowerment and environmental stewardship. We believe that true transformation requires both human and environmental sustainability.

Transformation

We are dedicated to transforming lives — not just through education, but through holistic community development. We measure our success by the positive change we create in people's lives.

Innovation

We embrace innovative approaches like TaRL and Learner Tracking Systems to solve complex educational challenges. We are always learning, adapting, and improving our methods.
