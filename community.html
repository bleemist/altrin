Community outreach

Radio Talks on Biiso FM 98.7

ALTRIN's desire is to work collectively with the community and general public to ease information access and transparency. In October 2021, we held our first radio talk show on Biiso FM 98.7 to create awareness of our existence and sensitize the general public on what impact and positive change we were about to create.

The public was enthusiastic about the new project that intended to inspire academic and professional excellence. Since then, ALTRIN has had open engagements with the community through various programs like radio announcements and advertisements about activities and programs that foster community transformation.

Radio Talk Schedule:

  • Fortnightly on Tuesdays
  • Time: 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM EAT
  • Station: Biiso FM 98.7
  • Topics: Education, career guidance, mentorship, parent empowerment, climate action

Volunteer With ALTRIN

Serving with ALTRIN is an opportunity to voluntarily participate in a community transformation effort towards inspiring academic and professional excellence in Buliisa District and Uganda at large.
