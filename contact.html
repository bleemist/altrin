Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I volunteer with ALTRIN?
You can volunteer with ALTRIN by filling out the volunteer form on our website or contacting us directly via email or phone. We welcome volunteers with diverse skills and backgrounds.
What schools does ALTRIN work with?
ALTRIN currently partners with 10+ schools across Buliisa District. We work with both primary and secondary schools, implementing our Learner Tracking, TaRL, Career Guidance, and Mentorship programs.
How can I partner with ALTRIN?
We welcome partnerships with schools, local governments, NGOs, and private organizations. To explore partnership opportunities, please reach out to us via email at info@altrin.org or call us on +256 (0) 793 662 118.
What is TaRL and how does it work?
TaRL stands for Teaching at the Right Level — a pedagogical approach that groups learners by ability rather than age. It focuses on foundational literacy and numeracy skills, with regular assessments and regrouping to ensure every child progresses.
How can I support ALTRIN financially?
Financial contributions help us expand our programs and reach more learners. To support ALTRIN financially, please contact us directly to discuss donation options, sponsorship opportunities, or partnership arrangements.
