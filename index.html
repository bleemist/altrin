ALTRIN's mission is to transform lives by targeting primary & secondary learners, undergraduates, and fresh graduates through innovative projects like Learner Tracking Systems, Teaching at the Right Level (TaRL), career guidance, mentorship, and parent economic empowerment programs.

What we do

Flagship initiatives for holistic growth

Learner Tracking Systems

P.1 enrolment → university graduation

Minimize school dropout rates and improve graduation rates by tracking academic progress, attendance, and at-risk indicators across key transition years.

Career Guidance

Academic counselling that accelerates ambitions, helping secondary students make informed career choices through aptitude assessments and role-model sessions.

Academic Competitions

Debates, poem & essay writing to sharpen presentation, public speaking, and formal argument skills — unlocking confidence beyond textbooks.

Teaching at the Right Level

Proven approach grouping learners by ability rather than age, improving foundational literacy and numeracy for targeted impact.

Mentorship Program

Connecting students to professionals through mentor-mentee relationships, career workshops, internships, and post-graduate placements.

Parents & Teachers Capacity Building

Teacher workshops in modern teaching techniques + income-generating projects for parents to meet children's educational needs.

Teaching at the Right Level (TaRL)

Identify foundational gaps and group learners by ability — proven to raise literacy & numeracy outcomes in under-resourced schools. Our goal: move every child to grade-level mastery.
