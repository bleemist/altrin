Teaching at the Right Level (TaRL)

Identify foundational gaps and group learners by ability — proven to raise literacy & numeracy outcomes in under-resourced schools. Our goal: move every child to grade-level mastery.

Strengthening Families, Securing Futures

We empower economic sustainability of parents through projects that strengthen financial capacities to meet educational needs for their children. From VSLA groups to small business incubation, ALTRIN works alongside parents.

ALTRIN is positioning itself to drive meaningful change through a focused, outcomes-oriented approach by addressing key challenges with innovative solutions and forging strategic partnerships to create sustainable impact while building organizational capacity and resilience.
