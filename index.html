What we do

Flagship initiatives for holistic growth

Learner Tracking Systems

P.1 enrolment → university graduation

Aims at tracking learners' progress from P.1 enrolment through to university graduation. The goal is to minimize school dropout rates and improve graduation rates.

Minimize school dropout rates and improve graduation rates by tracking academic progress, attendance, and at-risk indicators across key transition years.

Career Guidance

Academic counselling that accelerates ambitions, helping secondary students make informed career choices through aptitude assessments and role-model sessions.

Academic Competitions

Debates, poem & essay writing to sharpen presentation, public speaking, and formal argument skills — unlocking confidence beyond textbooks.

Teaching at the Right Level

Proven approach grouping learners by ability rather than age, improving foundational literacy and numeracy for targeted impact.

Mentorship Program

Connecting students to professionals through mentor-mentee relationships, career workshops, internships, and post-graduate placements.

Parents & Teachers Capacity Building

Teacher workshops in modern teaching techniques + income-generating projects for parents to meet children's educational needs.

Fortnightly on Tuesdays (Radio Talks)

Live sessions, expert talks, peer support & networking. Open to students, graduates, parents & educators.

ALTRIN's desire is to work collectively with the community and general public to ease information access and transparency. In October, 2021, we held a radio talk show on Biiso FM 98.7 to create awareness of our existence and sensitize the general public on what impact and positive change we were about to create/bring. This was possible through our partnership with Dolica SDA Development Project. The public was enthusiastic about the new project that intended to inspire academic and professional excellence. Since then, ALTRIN has had open engagements with the community through various programs like radio announcements and advertisements about activities and programs that foster community transformation.
